About

Who I am & what I build

With a foundation spanning full-stack web development, AI/ML engineering, and infrastructure management, I focus on building tools that solve real problems without unnecessary complexity. My work began at Gyanmanjari Innovative University, where I architected a university-wide ERP serving 4000+ daily users, led blockchain research presented at Harvard, and deployed custom AI systems across the institution — all while managing the production infrastructure they ran on.

My recent work leans toward AI tooling: libraries that give LLMs structured, safe access to databases (SQLPilot), knowledge base systems that eliminate hallucination (Basalt), and agentic workflow builders for ComfyUI (Comfy Architect). I build these because I use them, and I keep building because there is always a better way to make something work.

Outside of AI tooling I maintain a portfolio of self-hosted systems — a media streaming platform with torrent, transcoding, and Flutter apps; a unified backup system with Google Drive sync; and a security daemon watching production logs. I think in systems and I ship in production.
